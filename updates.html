  • The versions were less amenable to auto-tuning despite recording with BGM.
  • Recorded at 16/48 sample rate.
  • Decided to use Recstar to speed up the process, but it failed at 96 kHz due to bugs that break the end of the take.
  • Late night session (past 2:00 AM) sparked an idea to create an additional voice version—like Automaton (disposed).
  • Utilizing a CV Romaji bank plus help from ChatGPT to learn Japanese vowels and consonants.
  • Employed speech synthesis and synths to generate consonants.
  • Extracted vowels from previous bank versions.
  • Engaged in "Frankensteining" to combine and create unique sounds.
