Description

Have you ever felt a voice penetrate your skin and touch both your ears and the depths of your memory?

The most compelling voices often seem to reveal something strangely human inside their polished synthetic texture.

Ilya Minin (Eli) describes the voice’s “luxurious and eerie bass” as the perfect fit for the songs with mysterious, ritualistic vibes. With input from eikton, the timbre became at once dark, memorable, and unexpectedly lively
